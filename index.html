Our Story

I-choo-choo-choose you!

Alex and I first met back in 2013. We went on one coffee date to the Second Cup on Spring Garden Road (which no longer exists) and had a walk in the Public Gardens. “So, do you want to do this again?” I asked. “Absolutely!” said Alex. “Text me.” I did not text him. He did not text me.

Fast-forward to summer 2014, when I was surfing Tinder following a break-up. I recognized Alex’s photo right away, and when we matched, I initiated a conversation. We made plans to meet up the next time I was in Halifax, as I was working a summer job at a paper in Bridgewater at the time. We agreed to date casually at first, but then we fell in love. The rest – as they say – is history.

Some people think dating apps are unromantic, but without one, we may never have found each other again. I do think we were meant to find each other again.

-Becky
